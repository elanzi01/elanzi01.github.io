A Biography of Elizabeth Lanzilla

I am an undergraduate student at Tufts University currently pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Psychology with a minor in History, concentration being women and gender inequality struggles in the United States through the 20th century. I have also pursued relevant coursework in the field of Economics. Interests include consulting, technology, marketing, advertising, and non-profit work.

At Tufts University, I am a consultant for Tufts University 180 Degrees Consulting, a consulting firm internationally acclaimed for its work with non-profits and social enterprises. Through Imaginet at Tufts University, I enhance my knowledge of the marketing and advertising field through education, pro-bono work for university clubs and organizations, as well as agency visits. I am enthusiastic about creating social change through destigmatizing mental health issues around campus in Active Minds and mentoring a young girl I have come to know as my little sister through Big Brother Big Sister of Greater Boston.

I am passionate about approaching the world of technology, web design, consulting, marketing, advertising, and social enterprises with a creative mindset, entrepreneurial spirit, and strong people skills. In my free time, I enjoy poetry, fashion design, art, and theater.
